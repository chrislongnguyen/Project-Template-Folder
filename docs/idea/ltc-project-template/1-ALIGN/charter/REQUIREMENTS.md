## Decomposed Using Ultimate Truth #3: Verb + Adverbs + Noun + Adjectives

---

## How to Read This Document
Every requirement is decomposed into 4 elements:
- **Verb** (The Action): What must be done?
- **Adverbs** (The Outcome Quality): How well must it be done?
- **Noun** (The Subject/Tools/Environment): What is being acted upon?
- **Adjectives** (The Effectiveness): How effective must the subject be?
