# DISCUSSION SOP
## How to Discuss Effectively
### Derived From: Wiki — Discussion SOP

---

## Core Principles
1. **Premises over opinions** — Support your position with evidence, not just feelings
2. **Psychological safety** — Everyone can speak without fear of punishment
3. **Two-vote system** — Disagree and commit; don't block progress indefinitely

## Discussion Structure
1. **Frame the problem** — What are we deciding? What's at stake?
2. **Present evidence** — Facts first, then interpretations
3. **Explore alternatives** — At least 2 options, with trade-offs for each
4. **Check biases** — Is the group falling into groupthink? Assign a devil's advocate
5. **Decide** — Use the 3 Pillars as prioritization criteria
6. **Document** — Record the decision in an ADR with rationale
