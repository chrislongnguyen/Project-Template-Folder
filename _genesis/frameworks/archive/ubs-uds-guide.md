# UBS/UDS ANALYSIS GUIDE
## How to Identify Blockers and Drivers
### Derived From: Ultimate Truth #2; Section 3 — Diagnostic Tables

---

## Core Concept
Every outcome is shaped by two opposing forces:
- **UBS (Ultimate Blocking System):** The collection of blockers that hinder the desired outcome
- **UDS (Ultimate Driving System):** The collection of drivers that propel the desired outcome

**Key Insight:** The UBS exerts STRONGER influence than the UDS on valuable outcomes.
Therefore: manage risks first, maximize output second.

## How to Perform UBS Analysis (Trace Blockers to Root Causes)

### Step 1: Identify the Surface Blocker
"What is directly preventing the desired outcome?"

### Step 2: Trace to Root Driver of the Blocker
"What drives/enables this blocker?" (UBS.UD)
"What principles make the blocker work?" (UBS.UD.Principles)

### Step 3: Trace What Prevents Overcoming the Blocker
"What prevents us from fixing this?" (UBS.UB)
"What principles prevent the fix?" (UBS.UB.Principles)

### Step 4: Design Root-Cause Resolution
Target the ROOT PRINCIPLE (UBS.UD.Principles), not the symptom.
A mitigation that only addresses the surface blocker will fail when conditions change.

## How to Perform UDS Analysis (Trace Drivers to Root Enablers)

### Step 1: Identify the Surface Driver
"What is directly enabling the desired outcome?"

### Step 2: Trace to Root Enabler
"What makes this driver effective?" (UDS.UD)
"What principles make it work?" (UDS.UD.Principles)

### Step 3: Identify Vulnerabilities
"What could weaken this driver?" (UDS.UB)

### Step 4: Design Leverage Strategy
Exploit the ROOT PRINCIPLE (UDS.UD.Principles), not just copy tactics.

## Quick Reference — The 6-Layer Deep Trace

| Layer | UBS (Blockers) | UDS (Drivers) |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| 0 | What blocks success? | What drives success? |
| 1 (UB) | What prevents overcoming the blocker? | What can weaken the driver? |
| 2 (UD) | What enables the blocker? | What enables the driver? |
| 3 (Principles) | Why does it work/fail? | Why does it work/fail? |
| 4 (Environment) | What conditions sustain it? | What conditions support it? |
| 5 (Tools) | What tools enable it? | What tools power it? |
| 6 (Procedure) | What process leads to it? | How to exploit it? |
