# ZONE 4 — IMPROVE (Learn & Grow — Loops Back to ALIGN)

> "What worked? What didn't? How do we make it stick?"

**Workstreams:** All feed back here (Continuous Improvement)
**Derived From:** Ultimate Truth #10 (Continuous Improvement); Agile SOPs 2.6-2.7; Step 6 Consolidate & Institutionalize

---

## Purpose

Nothing ships without a review. Nothing improves without a retrospective.
This zone closes the loop: ALIGN → PLAN → EXECUTE → **IMPROVE** → (back to ALIGN).
Output from this zone feeds back into `1-ALIGN/` for the next iteration.

```
  ┌─────────┐     ┌─────────┐     ┌─────────┐     ┌─────────┐
  │ 1-ALIGN │────►│ 2-PLAN  │────►│3-EXECUTE│────►│4-IMPROVE│
  └─────────┘     └─────────┘     └─────────┘     └────┬────┘
       ▲                                                │
       │         Feedback, lessons, metrics              │
       └────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Contents

| Subfolder | Contains | Key Question |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| `reviews/` | Sprint/execution reviews | Did we deliver what we promised? |
| `retrospectives/` | Team reflection & improvement | How can we work better? |
| `risk-log/` | Living risk log — events + near misses | What actually happened vs. predicted? |
| `metrics/` | Effectiveness metrics (3 pillars) | Are we improving over time? |
| `changelog/` | Version history & institutionalized improvements | What changed and is it permanent? |

## Improvement Checklist
- [ ] Sprint review completed — stakeholder feedback captured
- [ ] Retrospective completed — UDS/UBS analysis of what worked/didn't
- [ ] Shadow operating systems detected and flagged (M-09)
- [ ] Risk events and near misses logged
- [ ] Metrics updated against 3 pillars
- [ ] Improvements institutionalized (Grounding → Sustaining)
- [ ] CHANGELOG.md updated

## Pre-Flight — 3 Pillars Check

### Sustainability — Are our improvements durable?
- [ ] Changes embedded into SOPs, not just verbal agreements
- [ ] Onboarding materials updated for new contributors
- [ ] KPIs have review triggers (not just static targets)

### Efficiency — Are we improving efficiently?
- [ ] Retrospectives are time-boxed and action-oriented
- [ ] Improvements target root causes (UBS.UD.Principles), not symptoms
- [ ] No bureaucratic overhead from improvement process itself

### Scalability — Do our improvements scale?
- [ ] Lessons are captured in `_genesis/` for cross-project benefit
- [ ] Templates and SOPs are updated, not just individual behavior
- [ ] Automation implemented where human repetition was the bottleneck
